Review Your Contracts.

Please read and agree to all four contracts below before accessing your dashboard. These agreements govern your working relationship with FidModels Agency.

I
Talent Representation Agreement
Agency terms, commission structure & exclusivity
▼
This Talent Representation Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between FidModels Agency ("Agency") and the undersigned model ("Talent"). The Agency agrees to represent the Talent exclusively within the Nigerian modelling market for a period of twelve (12) months, with optional renewal by mutual consent.

The Agency shall receive a commission of fifteen percent (15%) of all gross earnings secured through Agency bookings. The Talent retains full rights to independently secured work outside Agency channels, provided such work does not conflict with existing Agency bookings or exclusive brand deals negotiated on the Talent's behalf.

The Talent agrees to maintain professional conduct at all times, attend all confirmed bookings punctually, and notify the Agency of any scheduling conflicts with a minimum of 48 hours notice. Failure to honour confirmed bookings without adequate notice may result in financial penalties as outlined in Schedule B of this Agreement.
II
Image & Content Rights Agreement
Usage rights, licensing & intellectual property
▼
This Image & Content Rights Agreement governs the use of the Talent's likeness, image, and any content produced during Agency-arranged bookings. The Talent grants the Agency and its client brands a non-exclusive, royalty-free licence to use images and video footage captured during official bookings for the duration agreed in each individual campaign brief.

Any usage beyond the agreed scope — including but not limited to additional platforms, extended duration, or international markets — shall require renegotiation and additional compensation. The Talent retains the right to use behind-the-scenes content and approved campaign imagery for personal portfolio purposes.

The Talent agrees not to commercially exploit Agency-produced content independently without prior written consent. All raw, unedited files remain the property of the commissioning client or photographer as outlined in individual shoot agreements.
III
Code of Conduct & Professionalism Policy
Behaviour standards, social media guidelines & compliance
▼
FidModels Agency maintains a strict Code of Conduct to protect the reputation of its roster and the integrity of client relationships. The Talent agrees to conduct themselves with professionalism and respect in all Agency-related interactions, on and off set.

Social media activity that could be deemed offensive, controversial, or damaging to client brands must be avoided during active campaign periods. The Talent agrees to disclose any social media posts referencing Agency clients or campaigns to the Agency's PR team prior to publishing, for approval.

The use of illegal substances, engagement in criminal activity, or behaviour that brings the Agency or its clients into disrepute may result in immediate termination of this agreement without financial liability to the Agency. All disputes shall be resolved through mediation before legal proceedings are initiated.
IV
Payment Terms & Financial Agreement
Payment schedule, tax obligations & withdrawal policy
▼
All payments to Talent shall be processed within fourteen (14) business days of the Agency receiving cleared funds from the commissioning client. Payments shall be made via direct bank transfer to the account details registered on the Talent's profile.

The Talent is solely responsible for declaring and paying any applicable taxes on income earned through Agency bookings. FidModels Agency shall provide monthly earnings statements to assist with tax documentation upon request.

Minimum withdrawal threshold is ₦5,000. Withdrawal requests submitted before 3PM on business days shall be processed same day. Requests submitted after 3PM or on weekends shall be processed the following business day. The Agency reserves the right to withhold payment pending resolution of any outstanding contractual disputes.
